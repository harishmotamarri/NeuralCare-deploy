Core issue: Hospitals can see current bed and ICU usage, but they cannot predict future demand. Shortages are often identified only after resources are already full. A predictive system is needed to provide early warnings and enable proactive planning.

Our AI Solution

Forecasting that turns
data into early action

NeuralCare aggregates anonymized operational hospital data and applies multivariate time-series models to forecast resource demand 48–96 hours ahead. Every prediction is paired with a human-readable explanation — so administrators know what to expect and exactly why.

  • Multivariate Time-Series Modeling
    Random Forest Classifier and Regression Algorithm captures seasonal surges, cross-resource dependencies, and admission velocity patterns.
  • Tiered Early Warning Alerts
    Low / Moderate / Critical thresholds trigger alerts 48–96 hours before a shortage — when action is still feasible.
  • Privacy-Preserving Data Architecture
    All models operate on de-identified, aggregated operational data. Zero patient-level PII enters the pipeline.
  • Explainable AI Insights
    SHAP attribution exposes the top contributing factors behind each prediction — building trust and enabling targeted response.
